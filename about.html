About Us

Passagegold is a Travel Management Company established Eighteen (18) years ago to provide superior travel solutions to corporate organizations in Nigeria.

We are affilated with International Travel Partnership (ITP) http://itptravel.com/about-us/ , a global network of leading travel management companies with presence in over seventy (50) countries worldwide including Nigeria.

As an accredited member of the International Air Transport Association (IATA) we provide excellent services and support to our customers, specifically corporate, by continuously finding new and innovative ways to develop service driven technology to increase operational efficiency that promote the best possible business travel service and experience for our clients.
